Projects

Night To Day

In this project I used a CycleGAN to create two generators that transform images from night to day and vice versa. I used data scrapping to collect my dataset from timelapses of cities going from day to night. I then created several neural networks, the generator was a Res-Net combined with a U-Net and the discriminator was a simple CNN.
